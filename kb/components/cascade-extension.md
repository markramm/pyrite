Investigative journalism knowledge management extension for the Cascade Series. Provides specialized entry types for investigative work: actor, cascade_event, cascade_org, mechanism, scene, solidarity_event, statistic, theme, timeline_event, and victim. Designed for tracking complex investigative narratives with actor relationships, event timelines, and thematic analysis.
