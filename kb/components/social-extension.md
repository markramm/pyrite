Everything2-inspired community knowledge base extension. Provides writeup and user_profile entry types with reputation tracking, author checks, and engagement hooks (after_save_update_counts, after_delete_adjust_reputation, before_save_author_check). Includes custom DB tables for social engagement data and the social KB preset.
