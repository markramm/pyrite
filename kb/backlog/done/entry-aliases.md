Multiple names for the same entry:

- Define aliases in frontmatter: `aliases: [CIA, Central Intelligence Agency, The Agency]`
- `[[CIA]]` resolves to the canonical entry
- Autocomplete shows aliases alongside titles
- Useful for abbreviations, alternate spellings, common names

**Depends on:** wikilinks-and-autocomplete
